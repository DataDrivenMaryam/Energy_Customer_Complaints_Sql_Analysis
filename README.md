# Energy Customer Complaints SQL Analysis  
This project analyzes customer complaints in the energy industry using SQL, delivering insights that support improved customer service, operational efficiency, and data-driven decision-making.

The project simulates real-world tasks performed by Customer Insights Analysts, Business Analysts, and Zero Carbon Advisors in the energy sector.


##  Objectives
- Identify the most common complaint types  
- Determine which communication channels customers use most  
- Track complaint volumes over time  
- Evaluate resolution performance  
- Compare residential vs business customers  
- Identify unresolved or escalated issues  
- Provide insights that inform operational improvements  


## Tools Used
- **SQL(MySQL syntax)**  
- **GitHub** for version control & portfolio display  


## Key Insights
- Top 3 complaint categories  
- Channels that receive the highest complaints  
- Monthly and yearly complaint trends  
- Complaint types with long resolution times  
- Overall resolution rate (%)  
- Resolution efficiency per communication channel  
- Residential vs business customer behavior  
- Outstanding unresolved or escalated cases
